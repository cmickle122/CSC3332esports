Overwatch 2

Released on August 10, 2023 and is free-to-play.

Overwatch 2 is a team-based hero shooter. Each team has five players and allows players to select a hero with unique abilities. Heroes are divided into roles: DPS, Tank, and Support. Depending on the game mode, team compositions may restrict the number of each role available. For example, in competitive game modes, the team composition must include 2 DPS, 2 Support, and 1 Tank. Other game modes, may have significantly different rules and restrictions that are unique to the mode. For instance, "Mystery Heroes" from the Arcade playlist, automatically and randomly assigns a hero to each player which randomizes again each time the player dies and respawns.

The first Overwatch game gained immense popularity in the esports scene through its franchised league "The Overwatch League" starting in 2018. The Overwatch League was officially terminated in 2023, and one year later, the Overwatch Championship Series was founded. It is unclear how Overwatch 2 will fare in the esports scene in the coming future.
