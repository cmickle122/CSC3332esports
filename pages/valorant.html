Valorant

Valorant was released on June 2, 2020 and is free-to-play. It has 3 game modes: Plant/Defuse, Deathmatch, and Custom Games.

In Plant/Defuse, two teams comprised of five players work together to attack or defend designated points of interest on the map. A team can win the round if they eliminate all enemy players. This game mode can be played competitively or casually. Deathmatch is divided into two modes: free-for-all or team-based. The goal is to reach a certain kill-count before the timer runs out. In Custom Games, players can configure maps, game modes, and objective to their liking, and invite friends to play.

Players can choose to play as any of the 22 Agents available in the game, with each Agent having distinct abilities. It is up to the players to devise strategies and team compositions that will counter the enemy team. In some game modes, players are rewarded with in-game currency in order to purchase weapons, equipment, and abilities.
