League of Legends

Released October 27, 2009.

A game in which two teams of five players work battle to destroy the other team's base. Each player can choose a character, known as a champion, to play for the duration of the match. Each map is divided into three lanes, top, middle (mid), and bottom (bot), and has team-related structures to protect and destroy. The game boasts a massive roster of characters to choose from, each with their own background lore and unique abilities.

There are three different game modes: normal mode, ranked mode, all-random-all-mid. Normal mode is meant for quick, casual games, ranked mode is to increase a players rank in the game. The all-random-all-mid game mode randomly assigns a Champion to each player and are restricted to the middle lane.

League of Legends has been a major part of the esports scene since its release, with the League of Legends World Championship being one of the most prestigious esports events. The game also holds regional championship tournaments for North America and Europe, the League of Legends Championship Series and the League of Legends European Championship, respectively.
