Dota 2

Released July 9, 2013.

Dota matches involve two teams of five players. Each player selects a hero with unique abilities that compliment their team's chosen strategy. Teams must work together to protect their base and coordinate attacks on enemy bases and structures in order to win the game. Dota currently has 126 unique heroes and over 15 different game modes.

Defense of the Ancients (the very first Dota game), while not the first MOBA game, is often referred to as the most influential MOBA game that popularized the genre.

Dota 2 is one of the largest esports titles in the world and holds its major tournament, The International, annually.
