Counter-Strike 2

Counter-Strike 2 was released on September 27, 2023 and is free-to-play.

Players are divided into two teams of five, and each team must work together to complete an objective. Objectives range from rescuing hostages (Hostage Rescue) to planting and defusing bombs (Bomb Defusal) to eliminating the enemy team. The game mode Wingman reduces the players per team to 2. The game features competitive and casual modes for each of these game modes. Players use in-game currency to buy weapons and equipment to use during a match.

While Counter-Strike 2 is still relatively new to the esports scene, the franchise is not. In the past 20 years, Counter-Strike has been a major player in the esports scene, and known for its tactical gameplay.
