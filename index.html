Esports

Esports, short for electronic sports, is a rapidly growing industry involving competitive video gaming. Depending on the type of game, top players (also called pro players) compete individually or as a team in leagues and tournaments. Such events are often held in stadiums or arenas, streamed online to viewers in their homes, and broadcast on television networks.

The esports industry has attracted significant investment and sponsorships from traditional sports organizations, media companies, and popular consumer brands, further solidifying its position as a mainstream entertainment phenomenon. Some of these organizations include Red Bull, ESPN, and Cisco.

Depending on the game, the amount of game modes vary, but most include the option to play competitively or casually. Casual game modes are for players who want to enjoy the game without the pressure of winning or losing, while competitive game modes are for more serious players who strive to increase their rank and skills in the game.

Popular Genres

Multiplayer Online Battle Arena

Team-based genre in which each players play different characters with unique abilities.

Popular Games

  • League of Legends
  • Dota
  • Heroes of the Storm

Real-time Strategy

Player vs. player strategy. Players can take actions at any time and are not required to take turns.

Popular Games

  • Starcraft I & II
  • Warcraft III

Shooters

Games involving gunfights/firefights. Game types can be team-based or free-for-all and might be objective-based or a specified number of kills-to-win. Subgenres include first-person and third-person.

Popular Games

  • Counter-Strike
  • Overwatch
  • Call of Duty
  • Halo
  • Valorant

Fighting Games

Player vs player combat featuring blocking, counter-attacking, and combination attacks on a 2D plane.

Popular Games

  • Super Smash Bros.
  • Tekken

Racing

Similar to real-world car racing, players drive vehicles on a track. These games allow the use of racing peripherals (pedals, steering wheels, etc.).

Popular Games

  • Formula 1
  • Gran Turismo Sport

Sports

These games simulate real-world sports games, and allow players to control the players on a team.

Popular Games
